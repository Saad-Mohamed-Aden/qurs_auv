About Us

BLACK PEARL is our Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) designed for the upcoming ROBOSUB competition. Our AUV integrates cutting-edge hardware and software, featuring real-time object detection, machine learning algorithms, and precision navigation under water.

  • Hardware: NVIDIA Jetson NX Orin, custom sensor suite, and high-efficiency thrusters.
  • Software: ROS backend, computer vision, and AI-based decision-making systems.
  • Team: Led by Qurs AUV, a multi-disciplinary team of (CE-EE-ME)engineers specializing in embedded systems, software development, and underwater robotics.

The Project

BLACK PEARL is a cutting-edge AUV designed for navigation, object detection, and obstacle avoidance in underwater environments.
